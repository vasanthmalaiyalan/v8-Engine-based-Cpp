# 2-BY-2 ADDITION METHOD

Rule:

ab × cd

= (tens part × full number)

-   (units part × full number)
    

அதாவது:

46 × 42  
42 = 40 + 2

----------

## Example 1

46 × 42

= 46 × (40 + 2)  
= 40×46 + 2×46  
= 1840 + 92  
= **1932**

----------

## Alternative way

46 × 42

= (40 + 6) × 42  
= 40×42 + 6×42  
= 1680 + 252  
= **1932**

----------

## Break larger number

74 × 13

Method 1 (faster):

= (70 + 4) × 13  
= 70×13 + 4×13  
= 910 + 52  
= **962**

Method 2:

= 74 × (10 + 3)  
= 10×74 + 3×74  
= 740 + 222  
= **962**

# Final Summary

2-by-2 Addition Method:

ab × cd  
= ab × (tens + units)  
= tens×ab + units×ab

Simple. Always works.
